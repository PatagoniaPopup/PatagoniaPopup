About the Event

Immerse yourself in an authentic Argentinian experience curated by SHL Schweizerische Hotelfachschule Luzern students. This one-night pop-up brings the vibrant flavors and traditions of Patagonia to Luzern.

Authentic Cuisine

Handcrafted empanadas made with traditional recipes and high-quality ingredients.

Signature Drinks

Experience Mate like never before with our innovative cocktails and fine Argentine wines.

Live Music

Ambient sounds of Argentina will transport you to the streets of Buenos Aires.

Prices To Win

There will be a lottery where you can win amazing prices with each empanada you purchase.

What to Expect

A Night in Argentina

Our pop-up event recreates the warmth and hospitality of an Argentinian gathering. Enjoy à la carte finger food while mingling with fellow culture and food enthusiasts.

Grand Prize Lottery

Every empanada you buy automatically enters you into a lottery for a chance to win an exclusive trip for two.

Sustainable Practices

Cultural Immersion

From the decor to the music, every detail is designed to transport you to Argentina.
